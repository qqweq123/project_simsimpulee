# HOLOGRAPHIC SOUL (Glassmorphism) Style Guide

## Concept
- **Keywords**: Ethereal, Dreamy, Soft, Premium
- **Mood**: 꿈속을 걷는 듯한, 신비롭고 우아한, 미래적인 순수함.

## Visual Language
- **Material**:
    - **Frosted Glass (Blur)**: `backdrop-filter: blur(10px)`
    - Semi-transparent white layers (rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.2))
    - Subtle white borders (1px solid rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.3))
- **Background**:
    - Fluid gradients (Mesh gradients) that slowly move
    - Aurora colors (Pastel Pink, Purple, Sky Blue)
- **Typography**:
    - Elegant Serif (e.g., `Playfair Display`) for titles
    - Clean Sans-serif (e.g., `Pretendard`) for body
- **Colors**:
    - Background: Deep Blurple (#4c1d95) to Soft Pink (#fbcfe8)
    - Card: Glass white
    - Text: White (with text-shadow) or Deep Navy

## Effects
- **Glow**: Elements emit a soft outer glow
- **Reflection**: Subtle linear gradients overlaid to simulate light reflection on glass
- **Floating**: Elements gently bob up and down (floating animation)
